# Research metadata

**Project:** FightSafe AI

## Research focus

Research software for **software traceability**, **auditability**, and **governance requirements** in safety-alert review workflows, with combat-sports video as one validation domain.

## Potential paper topics

- Software architecture for auditable machine-generated alert exports
- Traceability protocols and export bookkeeping under partial observability
- Requirements engineering and design-science artefacts for AI governance
- Reproducible software artifacts for protocol-defined evaluation

These tracks support publication planning; this document is a living outline, not a commitment to venue or timeline.
